# Services Page – Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Objective
The Services page clearly communicates:
- What services are offered
- Who they are for
- What happens after booking

This is a **high-intent page** that nudges users toward booking.

---

## 2. Target Audience
- Users comparing healthcare options
- Visitors from Home page CTAs
- SEO-driven traffic

---

## 3. Functional Requirements

### 3.1 Services Hero
- Headline (e.g. “Healthcare services at home”)
- Short explanatory subtext
- Background color or stock placeholder

---

### 3.2 Services Grid
Display services as cards:
- Doctor at Home
- Nursing Care
- Diagnostics at Home
- Medicine Delivery
- Follow-up Care

Each card:
- Icon
- Title
- 1-line description
- “Learn More” CTA (non-functional)

---

### 3.3 How Services Work
Simple process explanation:
1. Choose service
2. Book visit
3. Professional arrives
4. Care and follow-up

---

### 3.4 Why Choose Us
Short bullet-style highlights:
- Speed
- Convenience
- Qualified professionals
- Home comfort

---

### 3.5 CTA
- Primary CTA: “Book a Doctor”
- Secondary CTA: “Contact Support”

---

## 4. Non-Goals
- No pricing tables
- No booking forms
- No medical deep dives

---

## 5. Success Criteria
- Easy discovery of services
- Clear next action
- Low cognitive load
