# Contact Page – Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Objective
The Contact page enables users to **comfortably reach Health Core** for:
- General inquiries
- Support
- Booking assistance

---

## 2. Target Audience
- Users needing reassurance
- Elder care decision-makers
- Users hesitant to book online

---

## 3. Functional Requirements

### 3.1 Contact Hero
- Headline (e.g. “We’re here to help”)
- Supportive subtext
- Clean background color

---

### 3.2 Contact Methods
Display clearly:
- Phone number
- Email
- Availability (e.g. 24×7)

---

### 3.3 Contact Form
UI-only form with fields:
- Name
- Phone or Email
- Message

No submission logic required.

---

### 3.4 Reassurance Copy
Short trust message:
- “We’ll get back to you shortly”
- “Your information is safe”

---

### 3.5 CTA
- Submit button
- Secondary CTA: “Explore Services”

---

## 4. Non-Goals
- No maps
- No physical address
- No chatbot

---

## 5. Success Criteria
- Low friction
- Clear contact paths
- Trustworthy presentation
