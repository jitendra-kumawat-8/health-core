# About Page – Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Objective
The About page should establish **trust, credibility, and clarity** around Ashokam Homecare.
It explains who we are, what we do, and why users can rely on us.

This is a **trust-building, informational page**, not a conversion-heavy page.

---

## 2. Target Audience
- First-time visitors
- Patients and families evaluating credibility
- Caregivers and decision-makers

---

## 3. Functional Requirements

### 3.1 About Hero
- Headline describing the mission (e.g. “Healthcare that comes to you”)
- 1–2 line supporting description
- Background color or subtle gradient (no real imagery required)

---

### 3.2 Mission & Vision
- Two content blocks:
  - Mission
  - Vision
- Concise, human-readable text
- Icons or typography emphasis allowed

---

### 3.3 What We Do
Explain core offerings at a high level:
- Doctor at home
- Nursing & diagnostics
- Follow-up care

Avoid operational or pricing details.

---

### 3.4 Values / Principles
- Grid of 3–5 values (e.g. Trust, Speed, Compassion, Quality)
- Each value includes a short explanation

---

### 3.5 Trust Signals
- Statements such as:
  - Verified professionals
  - Patient-first approach
- Optional placeholder testimonials (static text only)

---

### 3.6 Soft CTA
- CTA like “Explore Services” or “Get Care at Home”
- Navigates users deeper into the site

---

## 4. Non-Goals
- No team bios
- No hiring information
- No press or media content

---

## 5. Success Criteria
- Calm and reassuring tone
- Clear narrative flow
- Strong trust perception
